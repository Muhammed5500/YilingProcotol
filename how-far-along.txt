How far along is your product?

MVP/demo.

The core protocol is built and working. Five Solidity contracts — SKCEngine, QueryFactory, AgentRegistry, ReputationManager, and FixedPointMath — implement the full SKC mechanism on-chain: query creation, bonded reports, probabilistic stopping, cross-entropy scoring, payout settlement, and ERC-8004 agent identity/reputation. The protocol API handles x402 payments and orchestration off-chain; the math runs entirely on-chain.

We've tested 30+ queries end-to-end on Monad testnet with multiple Claude-powered agent strategies (Analyst, Bayesian, Contrarian, Sentiment, Calibrator, Devil's Advocate) that autonomously join, evaluate, and report. The mechanism works as the paper predicts — agents converge toward truthful reporting through game-theoretic pressure alone.

The project won 1st place at Monad Blitz — a competitive one-day hackathon where developers build and deploy working prototypes on Monad testnet, judged by live audience vote. Protocol documentation, landing page, and developer guides are live.

What we haven't done yet: mainnet deployment, real liquidity, or production users. The mechanism is proven in the academic literature (ACM EC 2025) and validated on testnet. The next step is mainnet launch on Monad and onboarding the first external builders.
